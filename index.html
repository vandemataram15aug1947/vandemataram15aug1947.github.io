Projects

Projects

Below are Selected Projects in Power Electronics & Embedded Control, Covering Single-Phase Grid-Connected PV Inverters with PR Control, FOC Control of Three-Phase Induction Motor Using SVPWM, DC Motor Control with ePWM, Gate Driver Circuit Design, Three-Phase Inverter Implementation, as well as Optimization & Monitoring Strategies in Smart Grid Systems.

Design and Control of Single-Phase Grid-Connected Inverter for PV Solar Application

Developed a Single-Stage Inverter System Integrated with Advanced Control Strategies for Efficient Power Transfer from PV to Grid, Including PR Control & LCL Filter Design. Optimized Inverter Control Techniques on TMS320F28379D to Ensure Superior Conversion Efficiency & Operation While Assessing Performance Across Diverse Environmental, Grid & Operational Scenarios.

Field-Oriented Control of Three-Phase Induction Motor Using SVPWM Technique

Developed, Analyzed & Simulated FOC-Based Control Techniques for a Three-Phase Induction Motor in MATLAB/Simulink, Integrating SVPWM Implementation on TMS320F28379D for Enhanced Motor Control. Designed & Refined Control Strategies on TMS320F28379D to Deliver Accurate Speed Control, Achieving Optimal Efficiency & Stable Dynamic Behavior Across Different Load Scenarios.

Design and Analysis of Isolated Gate Driver Circuits for Power Electronics Applications

This Project Demonstrates a Comprehensive Understanding of the UCC23513DWYR Gate Driver & Its Application in Inverter Systems. The Circuits are Designed to Efficiently Drive Power Switches in High-Frequency Operations, Offering Fast Switching, Strong Isolation & Enhanced Noise Immunity. The UCC23513DWYR is Essential in Power Electronics Applications Like DC-DC Converters, Inverters & Motor Drives.

Design and Analysis of Three-Phase Inverter Using Sinusoidal ePWM Modulation Technique

Designed & Analyzed Using PLECS and MATLAB/Simulink, Implemented Hardware on TMS320F28379D Considering Power Electronics Principles & Circuitry for an Open-Loop Three-Phase Inverter. Optimized ePWM Control Algorithms on TMS320F28379D to Achieve Efficient & Smooth Sinusoidal Output Waveforms & Evaluated The Inverter's Performance Under Various Operating Conditions.

Control of DC Motor Using ePWM on the TMS320F28379D Microcontroller

Designed & Optimized PWM Control Algorithms for Precise Motor Speed Control & Efficient Performance. TMS320F28379D is Utilized to Implement Precise Control by Modulating the Duty Cycle of the PWM Signals.

Integration of SOC and SOH Estimation for Li-ion Battery Management

Implemented Estimation Algorithm in Hardware for Accurate Assessment of SOC, SOH & DOD Parameters. Integrated the Estimation Techniques Into Hardware Systems Enables for Real-Time Monitoring & Analysis.


Optimal Operational Scheduling of a Grid Connected System Based on DSM

Solved the Demand Side Management Problem by Considering of a Grid Connected Network by Load Shifting. Reduced the Supply Side's Cost & Increased the Demand Side's Revenue in a Grid Using the DE Algorithm.

Overload Protection, Monitoring and Load Shifting of a 3-Phase Transformer
